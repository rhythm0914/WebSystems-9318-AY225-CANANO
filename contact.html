Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get a business permit?

Business permits can be applied for online through our Services page or in person at the Business Permits and Licensing Office at City Hall. Required documents include DTI/SEC registration, barangay clearance, lease contract, and valid IDs.

Where can I pay my real property tax?

Real property tax can be paid at the City Treasurer's Office, through authorized banks, or online via our payment portal on the Services page.

How do I report a public concern?

Public concerns can be reported through this contact form, by calling our hotline at (046) 434-1111, or by visiting the Public Assistance Desk at City Hall.
